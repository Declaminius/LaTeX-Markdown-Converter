Assume that $f(z)$ is $\Delta$-analytic and
$$
f(z) \sim (1 - z)^{-\alpha}
$$
for $z \to 1, z \in \Delta_1$ with $\alpha \notin \Z_{\leq 0}$. Then, the coefficients of $f$ satisfy
$$
[z^n]f(z) \sim \frac{n^{\alpha - 1}}{\Gamma(\alpha)}.
$$
