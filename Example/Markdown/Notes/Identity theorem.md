Let $G$ be a region in $\C$ and $f, g\colon G \to \C$ be two holomorphic functions. Let $D := \{\, z \in G \mid f(z) = g(z) \,\}$ have an accumulation point in $G$. Then it holds that $f \equiv g$ on $G$.
