The number $G_{0,n,2}$ of basketball walks of length $n$ from the origin to altitude two and never returning to the $x$-axis equals
$$
G_{0,n,2} = \frac{1}{2n+1}\sum_{k=0}^{n+1}(-1)^{n+k+1}\binom{2n+1}{n+k}\binom{n+2k-1}{k}.
$$
